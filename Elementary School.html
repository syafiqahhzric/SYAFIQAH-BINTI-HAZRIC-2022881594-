My Elementary School

Sekolah Seri Tunjong

I attended Sekolah Kebangsaan Seri Tunjong, a wonderful institution in my hometown. I studied there from Grade 1 to Grade 5. It was where I discovered my love for reading and creative arts, thanks to an inspiring English teacher. I also enjoyed participating in annual sports day events. The school instilled in me values like curiosity and perseverance, which I carry with me to this day.

Achievement

"During my elementary school years, one of my proudest moments was achieving first place in my class, which earned me an award from the school. It was a reflection of my hard work and determination. Additionally, I achieved a 1A in my UPSR results. While I believe there's always room for improvement, I consider it a stepping stone that motivated me to strive for better in the future. These experiences taught me the value of perseverance and aiming higher."
