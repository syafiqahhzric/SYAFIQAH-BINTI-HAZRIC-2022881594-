My Secondary School

Sekolah Tengku Suleiman

"I studied at Sekolah Tengku Suleiman from Form 1 until I completed my SPM. Those years were some of the happiest and most memorable moments of my life. The school had a warm and supportive environment where I always felt encouraged to do my best. My teachers were incredibly kind, always willing to help and guide me, and my friends were like a second family. Looking back, I'm grateful for the friendships I made and the lessons I learned during those formative years."

Achievement

"During my time at Sekolah Tengku Suleiman, I was proud to be a part of the school's library prefect team. It was a rewarding experience that taught me responsibility and leadership. One of my proudest moments was receiving an excellence award for my SPM results, where I achieved 6As. These accomplishments reflect the hard work and dedication I put into my studies and extracurricular activities, supported by my kind teachers and friends. I'm truly grateful for these milestones during my secondary school years."
